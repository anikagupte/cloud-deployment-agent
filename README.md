# Using Artificial Intelligence (AI) to Make Cloud Computing More Sustainable

In a survey conducted by PwC, 78% of business leaders said they have adopted cloud technology into most areas in their organisation, but more than half of these responses said they cannot yet see the outcome of their investment. 

In my current extended research project, I am developing an AI agent to automate the technical and administrative work behind cloud deployment for businesses. The tool will evaluate environmental impacts from two key angles, energy and water consumption, and then present the solution which encompasses both of these considerations as well as meeting performance and cost constraints.
